We Speak Too

Austin

Women, along with non-binary people, are woefully underrepresented as speakers at Austin professional events.

This is a list of women and non-binary people in the Austin area who are interested in speaking at tech and data professional events.

To add yourself to the list fork the Github repository and add your name to the JSON file or fill out the Google Form with your information.
