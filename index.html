We Speak Too

DC is considered one of the top cities for women in tech. Yet women, along with non-binary people, are woefully underrepresented as speakers at area professional events. This is a list of women and non-binary people in the DC area who are interested in speaking at local tech and data professional events. Let them know that we speak too.

DC women and non-binary people: Read why you should be on this list and how to add your information. You can either fork the Github repository or email me your information.

Everyone: Share this with women and non-binary people in your life that you think are or would make great speakers.

DC Tech and Data Event Organizers: Consult this list for your next event.
