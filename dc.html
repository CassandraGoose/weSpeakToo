We Speak Too

Washington, DC

DC is considered one of the top cities for women in tech. Yet women, along with non-binary people, are woefully underrepresented as speakers at DC area professional events. This lack of representation can have far-reaching effects. Marginalized groups miss out on opportunities to be presented as leaders or experts in their fields. They are less likely to feel welcome to such groups, missing out on valuable learning and networking opportunities.

This is a list of women and non-binary people in the DC area who are interested in speaking at local tech and data professional events. Let them know that we speak too.

To add yourself to the list fork the Github repository and add your name to the JSON file or email Kate.
