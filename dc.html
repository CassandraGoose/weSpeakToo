We Speak Too

Washington, DC

DC is considered one of the top cities for women in tech. Yet women, along with non-binary people, are woefully underrepresented as speakers at area professional events.

This is a list of women and non-binary people in the DC area who are interested in speaking at local tech and data professional events. Let them know that we speak too.

What can you do?

  • Add your name. Read why you should be on your city's list and how to add yourself. You can either fork the Github repository or email me. New to speaking? Check out the speaker resources.
  • Share this with women and non-binary people in your life that you think are or would make great speakers.
  • And if you're an event organizer, consult this list for your next event.
