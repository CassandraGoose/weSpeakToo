We Speak Too

Women and non-binary individuals are woefully underrepresented as speakers at professional technology events. We want everyone to know that we speak too.

Select a city below to see the data on women and non-binary speakers in your area and a list of individuals ready and willing to speak at your next tech event.

  • Austin
  • Washington, DC

What can you do?

  • Add your name. Read why you should be on your city's list and how to add yourself. You can either fork the Github repository or email the city lead. New to speaking? Check out the speaker resources.
  • Share this with women and non-binary people in your life that you think are or would make great speakers.
  • And if you're an event organizer, consult this list for your next event.


Want to add your city? Contact Kate Rabinowitz.
